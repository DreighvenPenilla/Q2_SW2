My Top 3 Favorite Dog Breeds

Picture of Dog Breed Name Description
Chihuahua
 If you ever want or need a tiny dog, you might as well get a chihuahua! Chihuahuas are fun little dogs. They are very entertaining and are known to be loyal to their owners. Each and every chihuahua differ in appearance, with some having long coats while others have short coats. They also come in different colors, ranging from light coffee-like colors to dark, deep colors like gray, black, and dark brown.
Shih Poo
 This dog is not just your typical poodle nor shih tzu-- this is a mix of the two breeds! These dogs are charming, friendly, intelligent, and at the same time very stubborn. Although these dogs can get big, they are good anywhere, whether in a large home or a small apartment.
Jack Russel Terrier
 These dogs are known for their extreme energy and intensity. They need sufficient exercise and training, or else they may act up. If you are able to perfectly manage this extreme fellow, you can reward yourself with a happy, loyal friend forever!
